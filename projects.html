Antahsagari

Antahsagari is capable of underwater motion in three directions. SLAM (Simultaneous Localisation and Mapping) is used for guiding marine movement and real-time mapping of its surroundings. Robot Operating System(ROS) forms the underlying framework for integrating various subsystems at the software level. ROS receives raw data from each sensor, which is processed by different components of the project for path planning and detection and identification of subjects underwater using SLAM and Image Processing.
